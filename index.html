About this project

Goal and Motivation

In most countries, we have grown so accostumed with ubiquitous Internet access through our mobile devices that we barely stop to think about it. This project aims to give a glimpse into the complex and somewhat invisible, mysterious technologies and infrastructure that bring the services and vast amount of information of the Internet to our fingertips.

To achieve that, our challenge was to make the invisible visible, and for that we chose to use Augmented Reality (AR), a technology for interactive experiences that combine real-world and virtual, computer-generated elements. This is exactly what was needed for the project: in the Interactive Experience that we designer, physical objects come to life virtually through the advanced interaction allowed by AR, and advanced computer graphics techniques expose layers of information that would otherwise be invisible.

This project was created by students of the Master's Program in Interactive Media Technology of KTH — Royal Institute of Tehcnology, for the course Advanced Graphics and Interaction.
